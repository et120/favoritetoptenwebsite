BRONEL'S TOP TEN

#10: LITTLE CAESER'S

Little Caesar's was at one point a solid choice for pizza for the price of $5, unfortunately as prices went up, and quality never improved, It is not worth the price for what it is. Another factor that makes little caesar's worse than #9, is the high inconsistency with their pizzas. At times you will have a solid pizza, and other times you will have complete, borderline inedible garbage (Ranging between straight raw dough, to looking like it has been tossed into the 6 rings of hell)

#9: PAPA JOHN'S

Papi Johnny's, this place is higher than Little Caesars because it is more consistent, though occasionally very geasy. The pizzas from Papa Johns are pretty good, but there is a reason why I ranked it so low. It is still pretty much your standard pizza with nothing very special, so with their pizzas being $12+, I wouldn't say it's the best option.

#8: PIZZA HUT

No One Out Pizzas The Hut, or so they say. #8 on my list, but not so much better than Papito Johnny's is Pizza Hut. Pizza hut is around the same price range as Papa John's, it's still standard pizza like I said. Though with this one I don't have the issue with it being occassionally greasy, I do have the issue with it being dry at times. Better dry than greasy, either way I don't go here anymore.

#7: ROUNDTABLE PIZZA

Pieology takes customization to the next level, allowing pizza enthusiasts to craft their own personalized pies. The fresh ingredients and unlimited toppings ensure that every pizza is a unique masterpiece. The quick service and modern atmosphere add to the appeal, making it a top choice for pizza aficionados who like to design their own creations.

#6: Best Pizza & Brew

Best Pizza & Brew lives up to its name with a delightful fusion of mouthwatering pizzas and an excellent selection of craft beers. Their inventive pizza combinations and quality ingredients make every visit a memorable one. It's the ideal spot to savor both classic and creative pizzas while enjoying a refreshing brew.

#5: Pizza Port

Pizza Port's reputation as a California pizza institution is well-deserved, offering a delightful array of specialty pizzas and an extensive beer selection. The laid-back, surf-inspired atmosphere complements the delicious slices, making it a must-visit for those looking for a quintessential SoCal pizza experience.

#4: Blaze Pizza

Blaze Pizza, with its fast-casual concept, brings the art of personalized pizzas to the masses. Their quick service, quality ingredients, and limitless toppings offer an exceptional pizza experience for those on the go. It's perfect for creating your own perfect pie with an array of flavors and textures.

#3: Little Caesars

Little Caesars' affordability and convenience have earned it a top spot. Their "Hot-N-Ready" pizzas are a quick and budget-friendly option, perfect for satisfying those impromptu pizza cravings. While it may not be gourmet, it certainly delivers in terms of taste and accessibility.

#2: Dante's Pizza & Cafe

Dante's Pizza & Cafe offers a cozy and welcoming atmosphere to enjoy their scrumptious pizzas. Their attention to detail in crafting the perfect crust and topping combinations makes each slice a delight. It's a hidden gem that deserves recognition for its commitment to traditional pizza quality.

#1: Artichoke Basille's Pizza

Artichoke Basille's Pizza takes the crown as the number one pizza place on my list with its out-of-this-world, signature artichoke pizza. This iconic slice combines creamy artichoke sauce, mozzarella, and a hint of garlic that's simply unforgettable. Their unique flavors, combined with a bustling New York ambiance, make Artichoke Basille's an unparalleled pizza experience and a definite must-visit for pizza enthusiasts.
